Horse


The horse is one of two extant subspecies of equus ferus. it is an odd-toed ungulate mammal belonging to the taxonomic family Equide. the horse has evolved over the past 45 to 55 million years from a amall multi-toed creature, Eohippus, into the large, single-toed animal of today.
